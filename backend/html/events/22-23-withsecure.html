'Sniffing keyboards (Turns out some of them stink)' (WithSecure Guest Talk)

Date : 2022-11-30

Difficulty : n/a

Delivered By : Miłosz Gaczkowski

Overview

Have you ever wondered if your keyboard is secure? Does that question even make sense? Join Miłosz from WithSecure in an exploration of wireless keyboards in this story-driven guest talk. Based on real events from a large educational institution, this demonstration will focus on the KeySniffer vulnerability. Despite first being disclosed over 6 years ago, a sufficiently dodgy keyboard can still be acquired from the UK's major computer hardware stores.

As part of this session, one lucky volunteer will be given the once-in-a-lifetime chance to wield a cheap no-name wireless keyboard, or to plug in a non-descript USB device into their personal machine. Don't miss out on this unique opportunity!
