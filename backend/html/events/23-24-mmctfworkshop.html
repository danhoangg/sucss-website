Member Made CTF Workshop

Date : 2024-03-06

Difficulty : Beginner

Delivered By : Sam Kitson and Jacob Racklyeft

Overview

As you hopefully know we have the Member Made CTF event on the 20th of March where everyone can take part in a CTF with the challenges made by all of you!

Leading up to this we are having a workshop on the 6th of March to help you with making challenges.

This workshop is for ALL levels of experience

Everyone is welcome and will have something to do, even if you are an absolute beginner!

  • We will walk you through how to create a simple CTF challenge
  • You will create your own Steganography challenges
  • We will use the Web101 codebase as an example, breaking down how to create a challenge
  • And we will be there to help you create other challenges for the MMCTFs.
  • We will also provide assistance and a space for those with more experience to develop challenges.

Making your own CTF challenges is a great thing to talk about during interviews and to put on your CV, so is taking part in a CTF event so make sure you come on the 20th of March too!

Once you have made a challenge no matter how easy or hard you can submit it and could have it featured on the Challenge Day!

Submit your challenge ideas here!

If you have already submitted a challenge please bring what you've done so far.
